Frequently Asked Questions
What is your band's availability? +

Our band is available for bookings throughout the year. Contact us to check specific dates.

How long do you typically perform? +

Our standard performance is 2.15 hours. We can take a break during when the tea is being served, or play through, the choice is yours

Can you play music during breaks? +

Yes, we provide background music during the break, let us know if you have any requests, we will be more than happy to accomodate you.

How long does setup and soundcheck take? +

We typically need approx. 60 minutes for setup and soundcheck. We aim to arrive well in advance to ensure everything runs smoothly

Do you offer a DJ service? +

Yes, we can provide a DJ service to keep the party going after our performance. This can be added to any package for an additional fee.

Can we see you perform live before booking? +

Yes, please contact us and we will try to arrange a date and suitable location where you can view the band live

How can I book your band? +

You can book our band by filling out the contact form on our website or reaching out directly via email or phone call.
