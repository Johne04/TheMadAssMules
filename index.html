Frequently Asked Questions

How long do you play for?

We play for 2hours 15 mins approx. This is also depending on start time

How long does it take you to setup?

It usually take around 1 hour from the top table is cleared, this also includes a quick sound check

Will you perform our first dance?

Yes, if the song is not on our list then we will need 3 weeks notice prior to the date.

Can you provide music for our drinks reception?

Yes, We can provide up to one and a half hours of light music

Do you also offer DJ?

Yes we do, the DJ can kick in immediately after the band

Can we see you play live before booking?

Please get in touch with a suitable date and we will try and organise this for you.
Please give us some notice and there should be no problem.
