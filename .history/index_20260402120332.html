Frequently Asked Questions
What is your band's availability? +

Our band is available for bookings throughout the year. Contact us to check specific dates.

How long do you typically perform? +

Our standard performance is 2.15 - 2.30 hours. We can take a break during when the tea is being served, or play through, the choice is yours.

Can you play music during breaks? +

Yes, we provide background music during the break, let us know if you have any requests, we will be more than happy to accommodate you.

How long does setup and soundcheck take? +

We typically need approx. 60 minutes for setup and sound check. We aim to arrive well in advance to ensure everything runs smoothly.

Do you offer a DJ service? +

Yes, we can provide a DJ service to keep the party going after our performance. This can be added to any package for an additional fee.

Can we see you perform live before booking? +

Yes, please contact us and we will try to arrange a date and suitable location where you can view the band live.

How can I book your band? +

You can book our band by filling out the contact form on our website, email us or contact us by phone.

Do you have a written contract? +

Yes, once your date has been agreed we will send you the contract link via email, then simply fill it in and hit the submit button and you're all set.

What happens if a band member is unavailable on the day? +

We have cover for all our band members. In the event that a band member is unable to attend, we will notify you ASAP. We can assure you that you will have a great night as we are fully prepared for events like this.

Can we contact you by phone to discuss any queries we may have? +

Absolutely! You can contact Shane on - 0851005374. If he can't take your call, please leave a message and he'll get back to you ASAP.
